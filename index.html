Quadruped Robot

Agile four-legged bot for terrain navigation. Specs: Processor - Radxa Rock 5c; Motors - MG995 (8 servos); Power - 5V 40A supply; Sensors - 2 ultrasonic, RPi Lidar C1; Audio - Speaker with custom LM386 amp circuit.

Humanoid Robot

Human-like bot for advanced interactions. Specs: Processor - LattePanda Iota; Motors - 12 MG995s, 10 SG90; Servo Hat - Generic; Camera - HuskyLens 2 AI module; Power - 5V 40A adjustable supply.

Desktop Robot
